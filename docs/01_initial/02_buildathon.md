## 🏆 How You'll Be Judged

Projects are evaluated in two phases: a community vote on demo videos, then finalist scoring by a judging panel across 8 dimensions.  The community voting will only be judged based on previewing your link and watching your demo video. 

<aside>

**Functionality** — Does it work?

</aside>

<aside>

**UI/UX Design** — Is it intuitive and polished?

</aside>

<aside>

**Creativity & Innovation** — Is the approach original?

</aside>

<aside>

**Problem & Solution Clarity** — Is the problem real and the solution clear?

</aside>

<aside>

**Pitch & Presentation** — Can you explain it in 2 minutes?

</aside>

<aside>

**Technical Complexity** — How ambitious is the build? Please note this is not how big is the build.  

</aside>

<aside>

**Business Potential** — Could this become a real product?

</aside>

<aside>

**Inspiration Factor** — Does it make people say "wow"?

</aside>

<aside>

**Pro tip:** A simple tool that works beautifully will outscore a complex tool that barely functions. Build small, build complete, make it shine.

</aside>
