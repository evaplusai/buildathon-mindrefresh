The Problem
Most people don't notice they're getting stressed until they're already in it. By then the body has been shifting toward dysregulation for 20 to 60 minutes — but the conscious mind names it last.
Existing tools don't solve this:
    • Wearables (Apple Watch, Oura) detect stress after it has already landed, give a score, and offer no intervention — and require expensive hardware on the body that many people can't afford or won't wear.
    • Wellness apps (Calm, Headspace) give the same generic breathing exercise to everyone, regardless of whether they're anxious, shut down, or steady. The wrong intervention at the wrong time often makes things worse.
    • Therapy is weekly at best, and most dysregulation happens in between sessions, alone, often at 3 AM.
The gap is real and underserved: there is no tool that catches the body's earliest signals, names the specific state you're in, and meets you with the right practice at the right moment.

The Product
MindRefreshStudio is a wellness companion that lives in your space. A small WiFi sensor in the room reads your breath, heart rate, and micro-motion — without anything on your body, no camera, no wearable — and detects when your nervous system starts shifting toward stress, shutdown, or overwhelm before you consciously notice.
When the body shifts, the app meets you with a personalized intervention drawn from somatic healing practice — not a generic affirmation, but specific language matched to your actual state and the moment you're in.

Core Features
    • Contactless nervous system sensing — Tracks breath rate, heart rate, HRV, and micro-motion in real time through WiFi signals already in your room
    • Four-state detection — Distinguishes regulated, rising, activated, and shutdown states; offers different interventions for each
    • Preemptive intervention — Catches the rising state before stress fully lands, when intervention is still easy
    • Personalized affirmations — User types one sentence about what's alive; app responds with affirmations generated in a healing-practice voice, matched to the moment
    • State-matched breath patterns — Different practices for different states (physiological sigh for activation, gentle activating breath for shutdown — never one-size-fits-all)
    • Pattern Mirror — Reflects the user's own patterns over time without judgment, building self-awareness
    • Return-to-regulated trace — Shows the user their nervous system actually returning to steady, in their own data
    • Trusted Witness — Optional one-tap message to a pre-chosen support person during sustained dysregulation
Structural privacy — Data processed locally on user-owned hardware; raw biometric data never leaves the home; optional Local-Only Mode for users needing maximum privacy
